Feeling Sad? 😢

It’s okay to feel low. Let these words and moments comfort you.

"Tears come from the heart and not from the brain."— Leonardo da Vinci
"Sadness flies away on the wings of time."— Jean de La Fontaine
"The word ‘happy’ would lose its meaning if it were not balanced by sadness."— Carl Jung
"Even the darkest night will end and the sun will rise."— Victor Hugo
"Every human walks around with a certain kind of sadness. They may not wear it on their sleeves, but it's there if you look deep."— Taraji P. Henson
"Sometimes, it's okay not to be okay."— Unknown
🌦 Cry it Out

It’s healthy. Letting your tears flow can be the first step to healing.

📝 Reflect

Write a letter to yourself. Be kind and gentle. Say what you feel.

💫 Light Ahead

Remind yourself: even storms end. Sunshine is never far.
